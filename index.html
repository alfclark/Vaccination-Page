Why should I get vaccinated?

In order do avoid any doubts about getting the COVID-19 vaccine, identify the following benefits of COVID-19 Vaccination:

Minimize the Spread of Virus

COVID-19 vaccine proved to help shape antibody response for immune system.

Developing antibodies

Vaccine has be proven effective to prevent someone from getting infected with COVID-19.

Protect people around you

The vaccine gives the benefit to minimize the ratio of infection, helping to protect the people around you.

Creating herd immunity

The vaccine also helps to reduce the mutation of the virus, increasing the chance to achieve a herd immunity.
